Shopify Domain vs. Custom Domain

Choosing the right domain is the first step in establishing your brand's digital presence. Below is a professional overview of the options available for your Aetheria boutique.

1. Shopify Managed Domain

Every store starts with a .myshopify.com domain. It is the foundation of your digital lab during the creation phase.

  • Instant Setup: Fully functional from the moment you sign in.
  • SSL Included: Secure transactions handled automatically.
  • Cost: Included in your base subscription.

2. Custom Professional Domain

A custom domain (e.g., aetheria.com) is the signature of a professional brand, evoking trust and authority.

  • Brand Recognition: Easier for your ritualists to remember and return to.
  • SEO Benefits: Search engines prioritize custom domains for botanical discovery.
  • Professional Email: Enables custom addresses like concierge@aetheria.com.

Strategic Recommendation

For a truly immersive, high-conversion Aetheria experience, we recommend securing a custom .com domain. This simple step elevates your brand from a temporary boutique to an established luxury authority.

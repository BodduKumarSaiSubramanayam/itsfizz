# Aetheria: Project Walkthrough

I have completed the foundation and implementation strategy for **Aetheria Botanical Skincare**, a premium Shopify store built for the Digital Heroes challenge.

## 🌿 Branding & Identity
Aetheria is positioned as a high-end, science-led botanical ritual. 
- **Palette**: Foggy greens and goldenrod accents.
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (Luxury Serif).
- **Logo**: A minimalist leaf icon with gold "A" integration.

### Aesthetic Excellence
- **Perfect 60/30/10 Ratio**: Implemented with Parchment White (#FCFAF7), Botanical Green (#1B3022), and Soft Gold (#C5A059).
- **High-End Typography**: Standardized with 'Playfair Display' (Serif) for headings and 'Inter' for body across all 18 pages.
- **Extended Storytelling**: Added 'The Botanical Philosophy' and 'Trust & Science' sections to provide a deep, professional brand narrative.
